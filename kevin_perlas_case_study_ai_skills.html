Case Study: AI Skills for Design Discovery  ·  ServiceNow Workflow Design Studio  ·  2025–2026
A high-performing team was struggling to scale. Their work was excellent and leadership wanted more.
THE SITUATION
ServiceNow's Workflow Design Studio had a 10-year track record of delivering results for ServiceNow customers and account teams. Engagements ran 10–12 weeks, often longer. There was executive pressure to compress timelines so the team could handle more volume without cutting scope or adding headcount. The team had tried various approaches and tactics over the years, but none proved to be reusable or scalable.
MY APPROACH
Leverage AI as an intelligent assistant. I shadowed two strategic enagements to assess where the team was spending its time, which activities required a human, and which could be automated. I prototyped a tool chain composed of 8 AI skills and tested it on a live client engagement. It passed with flying colors with a few opportunities for optimization. I led a team training and provided access to the tool set, and my peers were eager to apply the efficiencies for themselves.
AI skills hybrid diagram
INSIGHTS AND FACTORS
  • The bottleneck was the lag between process phases in which the team analyzed outputs and translated them to artifacts or provocations in the next phase.
  • The directive to "use AI" was org-wide. Nobody had connected it to the team's actual performance objective: freeing up capacity for strategy and client work, not just checking an "AI adoption" box.
  • Synthesis and documentation were costing hours that should go to strategy and client work.
OUTCOMES
Enabled strategists to reallocate their valuable time toward strategic decision-making and client management by replacing manual, time consuming synthesis and tracking with an encoded, AI-driven methodology they could plug into their existing workflow. Improved data retention and relevance by providing strategists with a reliable, traceable source of truth throughout the engagement.
My Specific Contribution
  • Designed and built the AI skill chain from scratch.
  • Successfully ran the first live pilot end-to-end and updated skills to optimize them.
  • Created and executed the plan to release the toolset to my peers.
What Changed
8
AI skills designed and built from scratch, encoding methodology proven to streamline work, surface themes, and improve traceability and accountability.
5/6
Peer strategists adopted at least one skill within 1 month of release.
80%
Reduction in manual synthesis time, which freed capacity for strategy and client work.
